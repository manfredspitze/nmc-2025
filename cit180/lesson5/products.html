Popular Mozilla Products

Along with the Firefox web browser, Mozilla offers the web community a variety of products designed to make web browsing and development safer, easier and more fun.

See the cards and table below for more information about each product.

Firefox Relay

Get secure, random email masks and a unique, masked phone number to use any time a website, app, store, or restaurant requests your information.

Relay users can choose between these levels of protection:

  1. Limited email protection
  2. Email protection
  3. Email and phone protection
  4. VPN protection

Mozilla VPN

Mozilla VPN helps you automatically block ads and ad trackers from seeing your online activity.

You can connect up to five devices.

Supported operating systems include:

  • Windows
  • macOS
  • iOS
  • Linux

Pocket

Pocket is a free service from Mozilla you can use to discover great content and save it for later with the click of a button.
